Jojo bizzere adventures

Jojo great. It's a really well crafted show, considering it's a manga adaptation. It rarely strays from the manga, and when it does, it never affects the story. It is deserving of its TV-MA rating, however, so viewer discretion is advised if you're watching it completely uncensored. Also, yes yes yes oh my god.

3. Johnny Joestar with Tusk ACT4
